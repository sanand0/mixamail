How do I use Mixamail?

First, add an email ID on the left.

From this ID, mail twitter@mixamail.com with a subject:

  • fetch: to fetch tweets from your twitter home page
  • update: to update your status
  • google: to search on Google
  • subscribe: subscribe to daily feeds
  • unsubscribe: unsubscribe from daily feeds
  • re: <status>: to reply to a status (available on fetched tweets)
  • rt: <status>: to retweet to a status (available on fetched tweets)

Why don't I get a reply?

Check your junk/spam folder if you don't get a response within 60 seconds.

How many tweets do I get?

At most 100 tweets since the last time you e-mailed. If you use multiple email IDs, it's fine -- the tweets will continue from where they left off for your ID.
